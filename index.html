How it works

Memorisation

Nomina Naturæ helps you to memorise the names and taxonomic organisation of species you've observed on iNaturalist.

Enter your username in Settings to get started.

Personalisation

Go to settings to personalise what you'll be tested on (e.g. only plants, or only latin names).

Spaced repetition

The app schedules each species review card based on how well you know it.

Ones you usually get right first time will fade into the background; tricky ones you'll see more often.

Tap on the homepage to view your progress across all species.

Your data

Your progress is saved locally in this browser. You can optionally sync it to the cloud — and across devices — by signing in under Cloud sync in Settings.

If you are not using cloud sync, clearing your browser data will permanently delete your progress.

Wikipedia

Tap ℹ after a species card is revealed to read a Wikipedia summary of that species.

Feedback & bugs

Use the feedback form in Settings to report a bug or suggest a feature.
